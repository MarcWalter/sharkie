Sharkie

Baby shark is busy collecting five coins, like every morning. Every morning since Sharkie can remember. Probably three.

He has never met the danger that Mama shark seems to evoke. Why should this time be any different?

But just in case, you can escape with the W-A-S-D keys or defeat the evil with your fin punch (space to hit puffer fish) or an air bubble (J to hit jelly fish). You need five poison bottles to defeat a killer whale. You don't need luck. You are strong, the terror of the seas, almost ten days old.

This game is only playable in landscape mode
